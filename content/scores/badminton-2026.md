## General Rules

- All players must report **15 minutes before** their match start time
- **Mavis 350 shuttles** will be used throughout the tournament
- **Toss** at the start of each match — winner chooses side or service
- Players need to carry their own **rackets, non-marking shoes, water bottles, towels**
- Playing barefoot or without non-marking shoes is **not allowed**
- **Food is not allowed** in the badminton court
- **Match referee's decision is final**
- First aid facilities are available at the clubhouse

## Scoring

| Category | Points | Deuce | Cap |
|----------|--------|-------|-----|
| Men's / Women's Singles | 21 | at 20-20 | 30 |
| Men's Doubles | 21 | at 20-20 | 30 |
| Mixed Doubles | 21 | at 20-20 | 30 |
| Boys / Girls Singles (10-16) | 21 | at 20-20 | 30 |
| Kids Singles (5-10) | 15 | at 14-14 | 20 |

After deuce, player/team scoring 2 consecutive points wins. Court change at midpoint (8th pt for 15-pt games; 11th pt for 21-pt games) with a 1-minute break.

## Format

- **Qualifying rounds** — Single game knockout
- **Semifinals & Finals** — Best of 3 games
- **Walkover** — If a player/team is absent, opponent advances automatically
- **Odd player rule** — In case of odd number of players, the player who won with the lowest margin advances; last qualifier decided by toss if multiple options exist
