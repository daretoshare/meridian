## Rules & Format

### Group Stage
- Round-robin within each group (everyone plays everyone once)
- Group A: 7 players → 21 games across 7 rounds (1 bye per round)
- Group B: 6 players → 15 games across 5 rounds (no byes)
- Both groups play in parallel; Group B players take a break / play friendlies during Group A rounds 6–7
- Time control: **5 minutes + 5 seconds increment (Blitz)**
- Scoring: Win = 1 pt, Draw = ½ pt, Loss = 0, Bye = 1 pt
- Tiebreaks: Head-to-head → Sonneborn-Berger → Most wins
